REPLACE THIS FILE WITH YOUR OWN CV (plain text).

The contents of CV.txt are injected into the chatbot's system prompt as
biographical context, so the bot can answer questions like "where did you do
your PhD?" or "what is your research area?". Keep it plain text — no PDF, no
Markdown formatting required.

A simple structure works well:

=== NAME ===
Jane Q. Researcher

=== CURRENT POSITION ===
Assistant Professor of Computer Science, Example University

=== RESEARCH INTERESTS ===
Machine learning, causal inference, sequential decision-making.

=== EDUCATION ===
Ph.D. in Statistics, Example University, 2021
B.S. in Mathematics, Another University, 2016

=== SELECTED PUBLICATIONS ===
- "A Title About Something Important", Conference on Things, 2024.
- "Another Useful Paper", Journal of Results, 2023.

=== CONTACT ===
jane@example.edu

(Tip: the full text of your papers goes in the data/ folder as separate .txt
files — not here. This file is just the short biographical sketch.)
